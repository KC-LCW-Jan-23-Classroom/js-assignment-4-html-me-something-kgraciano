Our trip to New York City

In July of 2022, my boyfriend surprised me with a short trip to New York City, New York. Although we were only there for four days, we had a fun experience. We all know how expensive it is to go visit NYC so, for most of trip we just walked and observed and I had the best time there. I now get to say I saw my first huge rat at the subway and it was terrifying! It was like I was in a different world. I've never been anywhere close to New York so, I am greatful I had the oppurtunity to go.

My willa

Meet my world, Willa. Unforturnatly, she was not allowed to particpate in this spur-of-the-moment adventure to NYC. Instead, she will be staying with grandma! Willa loves her grandma's house so we easily compromised. The reason why I am telling you about this because, grandma lives 3 and a half hours from KCI. Like the good parents we are, we sacrificed our sleep to head to grandmas the night of our flight. Our flight is at 6AM. Yikes!

KCI International Airpot

We arrived at Kansas City International Airport at 4AM. Perfect timing. We were rushed and yelled at by TSA, like always. With just one hour to spare we made it! At this point, we were TIRED but excited. We were both ready to roam the streets of NYC!

Welcome to New York City

Countless of people walking the streets New York City. I typically do not enjoy large crowds but, I found it admiriable just to watch people. Not to sound like a creep, I just find it interesting how different and unique people can be. The huge lit up billboards definitely captured my attention and was exactly how I imagined them to be. And Yes, Time Square has a unique smell to it not very flattering. You would eventually know when and where to hold your breath and cover your nose!

The best tacos ever

Although we didn't have a chance to eat at pricey high end, top notch, NYC's best restaurants, we did find a gem. Los Tacos No.1. I fell inlove after one bite. They make the tacos right infront of you and it's cool to watch how fast they can be. I had the best experience there and the service was great!. A long line out through the entrance everytime we visited, but for sure worth it. I recommended this place if you love tacos like me! Add this to your list when you plan to visit NYC! Manhattan and me

Since we were in NYC for only four days, we didnt have the chance to explore enough but it was much needed. We all deserve a little get away every once in a while. I appreciate my boyfriend for this little getaway. Can't wait to have many more!
